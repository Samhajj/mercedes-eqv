Charging and range.

When it comes to charging options, the EQV’s flexibility is sure to impress you: at home using the Mercedes-Benz wallbox or a regular household socket, and along your route using public charging stations.

45 minutes from 10 to 80% at a DC fast-charging station with up to 110 kW. And thanks to Mercedes me Charge, the charging process becomes something of a relaxing break.

1. Minimum charging time from 10 to 80% in optimum conditions at DC charging station with supply voltage 400 volts, current 300 amps; the charging time may differ dependant on different factors such as outside temperature, battery temperature and usage of electricity consumers such as heating. 2. Maximum charging power at DC charging station with supply voltage 400 volts, current 300 amps; the maximum charging power is dependent on different factors, such as outside temperature, battery temperature and the battery‘s state of charge when charging is initiated.

Electric Intelligence brings together charging, optimised route planning, driving comfort and efficient driving. Thanks to recuperation, a range of drive programmes, intelligent navigation with charging stops or pre-entry climate control, you are always up-to-date and in control of the available range.

Dares to be different, while ramaining attractive.

Refined design with EQ-specific elements – the first glance tells all: the EQV follows the Mercedes-Benz design idiom.

Expressive.

Design elements created especially for the EQ family – for example the black panel radiator grille – lend the EQV an electrifying, progressive character. You can personalise the design further if desired: for example with the AVANTGARDE Line equipment package, the EQV Exterior Design Package and attractive light-alloy wheels.

Space for well-being.

The modern interior with its very own EQ aesthetics is exemplary among people carriers, both in terms of its ergonomics and its high value appeal. This can be seen, for example, in the new MBUX multimedia system which offers a 10.25" media display with high-quality glass surface, as well as in the attractive design of the air vents and the generously sized trim elements.
